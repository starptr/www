Now that we know what [discrete derivates](/blog/discrete-derivate) are, let's see if we can derive (no pun intended) similar rules found in regular calculus.

## Product Rule

$$
\begin{aligned}
\Delta_h [fg](n)
	&= \frac{[fg](n+1) - [fg](n)}{h}
\end{aligned}
$$
